📋 Trip Summary

👪 2 adults + 2 kids (7–12) 🚗 Car 🏰 History & Culture 🍲 Local Food 👶 Family-Friendly 💰 $635

Destination: Kraków, Poland — round-trip road trip from Warsaw; 2 days / 1 night, one overnight stay in central Kraków

Dates: Sunday, 12 July 2026 – Monday, 13 July 2026

Party: 2 adults + 2 children (ages 7–12)

Transport mode: Own car, direct via the A4/S7 motorway both ways (~295 km each way, ~3.5 hrs)

Interests covered:
  • 🏰 History & Culture — Wawel Castle State Rooms and Dragon's Den, Rynek Underground Museum, St. Mary's Basilica, and the Cloth Hall bring Kraków's royal and medieval history to the whole family, rain or shine.
  • 🍲 Local Food — Traditional Polish milk-bar fare, pierogi, zapiekanka, obwarzanek krakowski, and artisan ice cream feature across both days.
  • 👶 Family-Friendly — Kid-friendly dining (kids' menu and kids' corner), interactive museum exhibits, and a flat, stroller-friendly riverside walk keep the pace relaxed for children aged 7–12.

💡 Travel Tips

  • Accessibility & dietary needs: No accessibility needs or dietary restrictions to accommodate for this family.
  • Day 1 vs. Day 2 pacing: Day 1 (93% chance of moderate rain, 15–18°C) is built entirely around indoor/covered attractions; Day 2 (25% chance of light drizzle, mild 16–23°C) shifts to the outdoor/interactive picks, timed for the drier morning before the drive back.
  • Dragon's Den access: The Dragon's Den's 135-step spiral staircase is steep and one-way, not stroller/wheelchair accessible — flagged for awareness, though no accessibility need was stated.
  • Getting around Kraków: All in-city movement is on foot after the family parks once at the hotel; Old Town is a pedestrian/car-free zone with paid street parking Monday–Saturday 10:00–20:00.
  • Breakfast on Day 2: The hotel is assumed to include or offer a light breakfast on Day 2; if not, budget contingency should cover an additional light meal.
  • Documents: This domestic Poland trip (Warsaw–Kraków) requires no passport or visa — standard photo ID is sufficient.
