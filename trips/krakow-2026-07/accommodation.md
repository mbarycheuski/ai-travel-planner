## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Property selection:** Chose Mercure Kraków Stare Miasto because it satisfies every hard/explicit preference simultaneously: (1) 4-star rating (confirmed 4★, 9.3/10 "Wonderful" guest score); (2) on-site underground parking, letting the family park once on arrival and not drive again ("minimal driving after arrival"); (3) a ~10-minute walk (~0.4 mi) from Main Market Square/Old Town — close enough to be functionally "Old Town-adjacent" while avoiding the Old Town's paid/restricted street-parking zone entirely; (4) a single "Superior Room" (double bed + sofa bed, 21 m²) that sleeps up to 4, satisfying the "1 family room ... suitable for 2 adults + 2 children" assumption from `requirements.md` without needing to book two separate rooms.
- **Rate used:** $115/night for the Superior Room (double + sofa bed) is a representative rate synthesized from multiple listings citing this room type at $111–$119/night; used $115 as the midpoint. The hotel's cheapest generic room type (not sized for 4) starts near $92/night, and its official/ALL Accor page did not itself publish a numeric nightly rate, so the $111–$119 figures (cross-checked across two independent aggregator searches) are used and flagged here as the pricing assumption to reverify at booking time, since Kraków summer rates fluctuate by exact date and demand.
- **Booking channel:** The linked page is the hotel's own official ALL Accor listing (verified reachable via fetch, confirms 4★ rating, family-capable room, on-site parking, and 10-minute walk to Old Town). Third-party aggregators (Kayak, Booking.com, Hotels.com) list the same property and were used only to cross-check the room-type nightly rate, not as the cited link, since the official hotel page is the most durable, authoritative source.
- **Alternative considered — 2 connecting/adjacent rooms:** Two standard double rooms at the same hotel (~$92/night each ≈ $184/night total) were also considered to satisfy the "or 2 connecting/adjacent rooms" preference, but this is roughly 60% more expensive than the single family-capable Superior Room for the same 2-adult-2-child party, so the single Superior Room is recommended as the primary, budget-conscious choice; the connecting-rooms configuration remains a viable fallback if the Superior Room is sold out for 2026-07-12.
- **Rejected alternatives:** Campanile PRIME Kraków Old Town (family-friendly, on-site parking) was excluded — it is rated 3★, not meeting the traveler's explicit 4-star-or-higher hard requirement. Qubus Hotel Kraków (4★, family amenities, paid on-site parking ~80 PLN/day) was considered but sits in Podgórze, across the Vistula from Old Town, requiring a longer walk/short drive that works against the "minimal driving after arrival" preference, so it was not selected as primary.
- **Budget fit:** At $115 for the one night, accommodation consumes well under half of the traveler's stated $1,000–1,500 total trip budget (which must also cover food, activities, and the already-estimated ~$104 in car-transport/fuel/toll/parking costs), leaving a healthy remainder for food and activities across the 2-day/1-night trip.
- **Parking cost flag for the transport plan:** The hotel's actual on-site parking rate (~120 PLN/night ≈ $32) is higher than the ~$20 mid-range placeholder estimate `transport.md` currently uses pending hotel confirmation (per its own Rationale note: "actual cost should be re-verified once `accommodation.md` confirms the hotel"). This $32 figure should be used to update the transport plan's Local Transport parking line and total in a future revision; this accommodation artifact does not itself edit `transport.md`.
- **No accessibility needs assumed:** Consistent with `requirements.md` ("Accessibility needs: none"), no accessible-room requirement was applied when selecting the room type.
