## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Single stop, single night**: Per `transport.md`'s `## Stops & Nights`, there is exactly one stop (Krakow, 1 night), so only one accommodation row is needed.
- **Property choice**: Hotel Wielopole was selected because (a) it is confirmed 3★ (verified via Expedia and Hotels.com listings), (b) its official site (wielopole.pl, fetched and verified live) explicitly lists a "Deluxe Double or Twin Room" with **max occupancy 4 / 20 m²** — the room type needed to sleep 2 adults + 2 children (ages 5 and 8) in one room without splitting the family across two bookings, and (c) it sits on ul. Wielopole 3, inside/adjacent to Kraków's Old Town, a short walk from the Main Square, Wawel, and the Straszewski car park already used in `transport.md` — consistent with the "near or in Old Town" preference.
- **Verification**: The official site https://www.wielopole.pl/en/ was fetched directly and loads correctly, confirming the hotel name, "In the very center of the old town" location claim, and the Deluxe (4-person) room type. Hotels.com and Expedia listings were also fetched to cross-check the 3★ rating and confirm a real, bookable Deluxe Double room rate.
- **Price and currency conversion (explicit assumption)**: Hotels.com's live sample listing showed a "Deluxe Double Room" (2-person) rate of **$72/night** for the property. Neither the official site nor the aggregators displayed a same-day rate for the specific 4-person family configuration (2 adults + 2 children) for the 17–18 July 2026 dates, so a **20% family/extra-bed surcharge over the base double rate** was assumed (standard Polish hotel practice for a 3rd/4th occupant sharing a larger room) — giving ≈$86/night. Converted at an assumed **1 USD ≈ 4.05 PLN** rate (representative of mid-2026 USD/PLN levels), this rounds to **≈350 PLN** for the family room for the single night. This price is an estimate pending live-date confirmation at booking time; it should be treated as indicative, not guaranteed, and re-checked against the hotel's own booking engine for the exact 17.07.2026 date before payment.
- **Parking (explicit assumption, reconciling with transport.md)**: Hotel Wielopole does not advertise its own dedicated car park (aggregator listings note nearby paid public parking only, reservation not guaranteed). Per `transport.md`'s stated fallback ("city parking if the hotel has none"), this plan uses the Straszewski Old Town car park already budgeted at 27 PLN in `transport.md`'s transport total — that cost is **not** repeated here to avoid double-counting.
- **Star rating / budget tier**: No explicit minimum star rating was stated in `requirements-v2.md` — only "economical, no-frills accommodation." A 3★ city-center hotel at ≈350 PLN/night for a family of 4 (≈87.5 PLN per person) is consistent with a budget-conscious choice while still meeting the family-room and Old Town proximity needs; a 1★ option (ibis budget Kraków Stare Miasto) was investigated but rejected because its rooms max out at 3 occupants (14 m² double + extra bed), which does not fit 2 adults + 2 children in one room without booking a second room — a worse fit for the "family room" need despite the lower nightly rate.
- **Accessibility**: Not required per `requirements-v2.md` ("Accessibility needs: none"), so no accessible-room search was performed.
- **Currency**: All costs quoted in PLN, the trip currency confirmed in `requirements-v2.md`, with the USD→PLN conversion for the sourced Hotels.com rate shown above.
