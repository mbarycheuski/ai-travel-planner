## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Route & distance/time**: Sourced from conadrogach.pl's route planner for Kraków–Łódź, which lists a fastest option of 275 km / ≈3h08m via A1/S1/A4 with a noted toll section, and a slightly shorter non-toll option of 236 km / ≈3h40m. We selected the A1/S1/A4 corridor (via Piotrków Trybunalski, Częstochowa, Katowice) and rounded to 260 km / ≈3h10m as a representative real-world estimate; both directions comfortably clear the 4-hour one-way cap, so no intermediate stop is needed.
- **Tolls**: Confirmed via official Stalexport Autostrada Małopolska price list that only the A4 Katowice–Kraków concession section (Mysłowice–Balice, ≈60 km) is tolled for passenger cars, at 18 PLN per gate × 2 gates = 36 PLN one-way (rate effective 1 April 2026). The A1 stretch from Łódź through Piotrków Trybunalski and Częstochowa is a public (GDDKiA) motorway and free for cars.
- **Fuel**: Assumed an economy/compact rental (e.g., Fiat 500/Kia Rio class, consistent with the cheapest class listed by rentcars.pl) consuming 6.5 L/100km. Fuel price taken as 6.90 PLN/L for PB95, the upper end of the 6.78–6.92 PLN/L range reported for the week of 13–19 July 2026 by cenypaliw.fyi (used as a conservative estimate).
- **Rental car rate**: rentcars.pl lists an average economy-car rate of ~90 PLN/day for Łódź; assumed a 2-day hire (pickup morning of 17 July, return evening of 18 July) to cover the whole 2-day/1-night trip.
- **Day-of-week**: 17 July 2026 is a Friday and 18 July 2026 is a Saturday (per calendar), used to select the correct krakowparking.pl weekday/weekend day-rates (12 PLN / 15 PLN).
- **Parking**: krakowparking.pl's Straszewski Old Town car park was used as the primary parking option since it sits within walking distance of the Main Square and Wawel; the municipal paid-zone rate (Subzone A, 6 PLN/hour, active only 10:00–20:00 Mon–Sat) is offered as a fallback if a specific hotel doesn't include its own car park — this will need to be reconciled with whatever parking the accommodation-planner's chosen hotel actually provides (per requirement's parking assumption).
- **No public/rail alternative considered**: per confirmed transport mode = car, only driving and in-town parking/walking are planned; local transport within Kraków is assumed to be entirely on foot given the compact Old Town.
- All costs are quoted in PLN, the trip currency confirmed in `requirements-v2.md`.
