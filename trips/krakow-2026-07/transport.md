## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Own car vs. rental — decision:** Assumed the family drives their **own car** from Warsaw rather than renting one. Warsaw is the stated origin/departure point, consistent with the family living in or near Warsaw and having a car available there; a domestic 2‑day/1‑night trip within Poland is a classic own‑car use case. This is explicitly flagged as an assumption to confirm with the traveler if they do not, in fact, own/have access to a car in Warsaw.
- **Cost check against renting:** Even a budget economy rental in Warsaw runs from roughly $9–18/day (excluding insurance add‑ons, deposit holds, and one‑way/return logistics since the trip starts and ends in Warsaw) — see [Kayak — Warsaw car rentals](https://www.kayak.com/Cheap-Warsaw-Car-Rentals.16954.cars.ksp). For a 2‑day rental (~$20–36) plus mandatory insurance, renting would cost more than simply using an owned car and would eat further into the tight $1,000–1,500 total budget, so own-car is both cheaper and simpler logistically. If the traveler does not have their own car, this rental listing is a viable fallback and keeps the added cost modest (~$20–40 for 2 days).
- **Single direct leg (hard constraint):** The ~295 km / ~3.5 hr Warsaw–Kraków drive is comfortably a one-sitting drive for adults and is not split into intermediate stops or an overnight waypoint, per the traveler's explicit requirement. No leg in this plan exceeds the ~3.5 hr one-way drive time.
- **Route:** A4/S7 motorway corridor (the standard, fastest route between the two cities); no border crossings, domestic tolls only.
- **Fuel consumption assumption:** 7 L/100 km used as a representative mid-size family car consumption figure (per TyreMap's route calculator), applied to the ~590 km round trip. Actual consumption will vary by specific vehicle.
- **Toll assumption:** Based on the current A4 Katowice–Kraków concession toll (PLN 18 per 2-axle passenger vehicle at each of the two plazas, Mysłowice and Balice); the Warsaw–Kraków route only encounters the tolled A4 section near Kraków, not the full corridor.
- **Parking assumption:** No specific hotel is confirmed yet (accommodation planner runs after this artifact), so a mid-range Kraków Old Town parking estimate (60–100 PLN/night) is used, based on published hotel-garage and private-lot rates; actual cost should be re-verified once `accommodation.md` confirms the hotel.
- **Minimal driving in Kraków:** Once parked, all in-city movement is assumed to be on foot (Old Town and Wawel are walkable and largely car-free), matching the traveler's stated preference for minimal driving after arrival — no local driving legs are planned.
- **Currency conversion:** USD figures derived from the PLN/USD rate implied by the cited fuel-price source (~PLN 3.77 = $1, i.e., $1 ≈ PLN 3.77, consistent with PLN 5.96/L = $1.58/L cited on GlobalPetrolPrices) and applied consistently to tolls and parking.
