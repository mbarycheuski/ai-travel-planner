## Estimated Activities Total

- Day 1: 50 (St. Mary's Basilica) + 90 (Rynek Underground family ticket) = 140 PLN
- Day 2: 32 (Dragon's Den) + 160 (Zoo family ticket) = 192 PLN
- **Grand total: 140 + 192 = 332 PLN** for the whole group (2 adults + 2 children) across both days

This leaves roughly **1,988 − 332 = 1,656 PLN** of the all-inclusive 2,500 PLN budget for accommodation and food (per `transport.md`, 1,988 PLN remained after the 512 PLN transport spend).

## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Pacing**: Day 1 is intentionally lighter (afternoon-only, since the group departs Łódź in the morning and needs ~3h10m driving to arrive), concentrated in the compact, flat Old Town/Main Square area within a few minutes' walk of each other and of the Old Town parking recorded in `transport.md`. Day 2 is a fuller day (castle grounds → Dragon's Den → riverside walk → Zoo) but stays moderate-paced with rest/play breaks, avoiding late-night or physically demanding activities, consistent with `requirements-v2.md`'s assumption of a family-friendly, moderate pace suitable for a 5- and 8-year-old.
- **No duplicate attractions**: each attraction (Main Square, Cloth Hall, St. Mary's Basilica, Rynek Underground, Wawel grounds, Dragon's Den, Vistula Boulevards, Zoo) appears exactly once across the two days.
- **Weather fit**: both days are warm and dry per `weather.md` (17 July clear 16–27°C; 18 July overcast 19–31°C), so the plan favors outdoor/walking activities (Main Square, Wawel grounds, Vistula Boulevards, Zoo) with one indoor, air-conditioned midday activity each day (St. Mary's Basilica, Rynek Underground Museum) to give the children a cool break and shade during peak afternoon heat; sun protection is assumed to be packed per `weather.md`'s recommendation.
- **Age-based ticket assumptions**: St. Mary's Basilica lists reduced pricing for ages 7–18 and does not name a specific under-7 free policy; I assumed the 5-year-old enters free (a common Polish attraction convention for pre-school-age children) and applied the reduced rate only to the 8-year-old. This should be reconfirmed at the door if precision matters. Dragon's Den and the Zoo's official pricing pages give explicit reduced/family rates for children, so no assumption was needed there.
- **Kid-friendly requirement per day**: Day 1 meets this via the Rynek Underground Museum's interactive multimedia format (explicitly noted as more engaging for children than a static exhibit); Day 2 meets this via Dragon's Den (explicitly marketed "for children" per krakow.travel) and Kraków Zoo.
- **No accessibility needs** were stated in `requirements-v2.md`, so stairs at Dragon's Den and St. Mary's Basilica's tourist entrance were not treated as blockers; flagged in the Suitability column for transparency in case this changes.
- **Currency**: every cost is quoted in PLN, the trip currency confirmed in `requirements-v2.md`; no foreign-currency source needed conversion since all cited attractions publish PLN pricing directly (Polish official/tourism-board or venue sites).
- **Budget fit**: the 332 PLN activities total is a small fraction of the 1,988 PLN remaining after transport (per `transport.md`), deliberately leaving the bulk of the remaining budget for accommodation and food, consistent with the "economical, no-frills" preference in `requirements-v2.md`.
- **Zoo timing/location**: Kraków Zoo is a short drive from the Old Town (outside easy walking distance); the group is traveling by car per the confirmed transport mode, so a short drive/re-park for the Saturday-afternoon Zoo visit before the return leg to Łódź is assumed feasible within the day's schedule and does not affect the Łódź return leg documented in `transport.md`.
