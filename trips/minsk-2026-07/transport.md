## Local Transport

**Minsk (Day 1 arrival 11:54 → Day 2 departure 18:11):**

- **Station connectivity:** Minsk-Pasazhirsky station sits directly at **Ploshcha Lyenina** metro station (Maskoŭskaja line) — no transfer bus/taxi needed to reach the city center. Source: [Minsk railway station – Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minsk_railway_station), [Ploshcha Lyenina (Minsk Metro) – Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ploshcha_Lyenina_(Minsk_Metro)).
- **Walkability:** Central Minsk (Independence Avenue corridor, Upper Town, Trinity Suburb, October Square) is compact and walkable from Ploshcha Lyenina — many downtown museums and the central parks are within a 10–20 minute walk. **If the hotel is on/near Independence Ave (per accommodation-agent's assumption), walking alone may suffice for much of Day 1/Day 2**, with the metro used opportunistically for longer hops (e.g., to a park further out) or if the kids tire.
- **Fares (effective from 2026-03-27):**
  - Metro token (жетон): **1.15 BYN** per ride, or tap a bank card at the turnover for the same price.
  - Bus/tram/trolleybus single ticket (талон): **1.10 BYN** (1.15 BYN if bought from the driver).
  - 10-ride metro-only pass: **9.5 BYN** (not recommended here — see below).
  - Source: [vgmbuh.by — Minsk transport tariffs from 27 March 2026](https://vgmbuh.by/novosti/skolko-stoit-talon-zheton-i-proezdnoj-bilet-v-minske/), [Minsktrans tariffs](https://minsktrans.by/oplata-proezda/tarify/), [Minsk Metro fares](https://metropoliten.by/information/fare/tarif/).
- **Children's fares on city transit:** Children **under 7 ride free** on Minsk city public transport (metro, bus, tram, trolleybus) — the 5-year-old is free on every ride. The 8-year-old **pays the full adult fare** (no city-transit child discount exists for non-enrolled visiting children; the school-ID-based free ride only applies to Minsk-enrolled students). Source: [Minsktrans — free/discounted travel](https://minsktrans.by/passazhiram/besplatnyj-proezd/).
- **Day-pass options:** Minsk city transport does not offer a cheap short-term (1–2 day) unlimited pass suited to a single overnight visit — the main multi-ride products are the 10-trip pass (9.5 BYN, metro only) and 10/30-day unified passes (tens of BYN), which are poor value for a ~1.5-day stay with heavy walking. **Recommendation: pay per ride with token/card tap** rather than buying a pass.
- **Rough local transport budget for the stay:** Assuming ~4–6 paid single rides total across Day 1 and Day 2 (e.g., one metro hop from station to hotel area, one or two hops to/from a park, one hop back toward the station before the return train) for **3 fare-paying travelers** (2 adults + 8-year-old; 5-year-old free throughout):
  - 3 payers × 5 avg rides × ~1.13 BYN avg fare ≈ **~17 BYN** for the whole family's local transport for the stay.
  - **Budget line to carry forward: ~15–25 BYN** (rounding up for a taxi/rideshare contingency if the kids get tired or luggage is heavy near the return train — not separately priced here; assume it is absorbed within this range or treated as an optional add-on).
