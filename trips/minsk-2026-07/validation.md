## Findings

1. **QG1 FAIL — over-budget default plan.** 
   - **Problem:** accommodation.md and food.md each present one option as their "RECOMMENDED" choice, and those recommended choices, aggregated in budget.md, total ≈587 BYN (87 BYN over the 500 BYN ceiling), even before considering the optional Kvantum museum. budget.md's own alternate ≈497 BYN figure depends on options accommodation.md and food.md do **not** currently recommend.
   - **Recommendation:** Per the execution plan's QG1 iteration strategy, actually update the implicated artifacts rather than leaving the trim only inside budget.md:
     - **accommodation.md:** Change the recommended option from Option 1 (Kirova St., 100 BYN) to Option 3 (Ploshchad Pobedy, 86 BYN), or explicitly document that Option 3 is now the adopted recommendation for budget-fit reasons (saves ~14 BYN).
     - **food.md:** Change the Day 1 dinner recommendation from Vasilki (sit-down, ~80 BYN) to the "Pizza (budget backup)" option (~56 BYN), and change Day 2 breakfast recommendation from the bakery (~32 BYN) to self-catering using the apartment kitchen (0 BYN) — saves ~56 BYN combined.
     - **activities.md:** Adopt the low end of the stated cost ranges for Gorky Park (15 BYN) and Central Botanical Garden (22 BYN) as the planning figures rather than the ranges' midpoints/high ends — saves ~13 BYN.
     - **transport.md:** Adopt the low end of the local-transport range (15 BYN rather than 15–25 BYN) as the planning figure.
     - **budget-agent:** Re-run once the above are updated, to produce a single, non-contradictory total (expected ≈497 BYN, consistent with the trimmed calculation already shown), and remove the now-superseded ≈587 BYN headline figure or clearly relabel it as a "before trims, do-not-use" reference only.
   - This directly resolves the QG6 finding below as well, since it removes the mismatch between "recommended" options and the budget-compliant options.

2. **QG6 FAIL — recommendation/budget mismatch.**

   - **Recommendation:** Same fix as Finding 1 — synchronize accommodation.md's and food.md's "RECOMMENDED" labels with the combination that budget.md shows actually fits the ceiling, then re-validate.

3. **Minor (non-blocking) note, not a gate failure:** Several core cost inputs remain unconfirmed at time of planning (train fare ~110 BYN, National History Museum ~20 BYN, Botanical Garden summer pricing, several restaurant per-person prices) and are already explicitly flagged as assumptions in their source artifacts with a 10% contingency applied in budget.md. Recommend the budget-agent's contingency be re-checked against actual booked prices before departure, and/or that 5–10 BYN of further headroom be identified (e.g., skip the pizza-backup upgrade to bakery, or drop one paid Gorky Park ride) as a standing buffer.
