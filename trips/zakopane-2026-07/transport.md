"split rather than exceed" rule — each direction is broken into two legs
with a rest/lunch stop at Częstochowa (home of Jasna Góra, an easy,
family-friendly stretch break roughly at the trip's midpoint). Each
individual leg is well under 3h10m, so no single driving segment approaches
the cap, and the family gets a proper break with 5- and 8-year-olds in the
car. Total driving time per direction (excluding the rest stop itself) is
~4h55m, consistent with both cited sources.

## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Own car assumed, not a rental.** The requirements say "own/rental car"
  without deciding between them; for a 2-night domestic road trip for a
  family of 4, using their own car is the lower-cost, higher-certainty
  default (no rental-desk booking, deposit, or insurance research needed),
  and it fits the tight 2000–3000 PLN budget better than a rental. This is
  stated explicitly as an assumption — if the family does not have access
  to a car, a rerun of this plan would need to add rental-company sourcing
  and daily rental rates.
- **Route choice.** Two independently-verified route-planner sources
  ([flagma.pl](https://flagma.pl/en/distance-lodz-zakopane/) and
  [rome2rio](https://www.rome2rio.com/s/Zakopane/%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA)) both
  put the drive at ~360–380 km one-way via S8/A1 south through Piotrków
  Trybunalski and Częstochowa, then A4 east–west link through the
  Katowice–Kraków corridor, then S7 "Zakopianka" south to Zakopane. This is
  the standard, fastest, non-backtracking route; no sensible alternative
  avoids the A4 toll section without a large detour.
- **A1 north of Częstochowa is toll-free for passenger cars in 2026** — the
  Gdańsk–Toruń stretch is the only tolled part of the state-run A1; the
  Włocławek–Łódź–Piotrków–Częstochowa stretch used here is free, confirmed
  via web search (search summary cited informally; the A4 toll figure used
  in the cost total comes from the directly-fetched, verified
  [wkatowicach.eu](https://www.wkatowicach.eu/informacje/w-drodze/Podwyzki-oplat-na-A4-KatowiceKrakow-od-1-kwietnia-2026-Sprawdz-nowe-stawki/idn:11841)
  source).
- **Rest-stop split at Częstochowa** is an assumption, not a hard
  requirement from the traveler — added because the raw one-way drive time
  (~5h10m per flagma.pl) sits at/slightly above the assumed 4–5h one-way
  cap for young children, and splitting it into two ~1h50m/~3h05m legs with
  a stretch break keeps every individual driving segment comfortably under
  the cap (QG2).
- **Fuel consumption (7 L/100 km)** is a generic mid-size family car/estate
  assumption (typical for a 4-person road trip with luggage), not sourced
  per-vehicle since the traveler's own car model is unknown.
- **Hotel parking cost (≈30 PLN/night)** is an assumption pending the
  specific hotel `accommodation-planner` selects; actual rate depends on
  that property. If the chosen hotel offers free parking, the transport
  total will be ≈30 PLN lower per accommodation-planner's rerun.
- **No separate local transit needed** — Zakopane's centre (Krupówki and
  surrounding streets) is compact and walkable from typical budget-hotel
  locations, so no bus/taxi budget is included for in-town movement. If
  activities-planner routes the family to Morskie Oko (a common family
  day-trip in the Tatras), the Palenica Białczańska parking-lot cost row
  above is provided as a placeholder; it is not counted in the transport
  total since it depends on the itinerary activities-planner produces.
- **Return date/time**: assumed the family drives back on 2026-07-14
  (the final day of the 2-night stay), consistent with the confirmed dates.
