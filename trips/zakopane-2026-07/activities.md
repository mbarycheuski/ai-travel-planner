## Estimated Activities Total

Assuming one round-trip Gubałówka ticket per family member and the low end of the ranges above (2 adults + 2 children; discounted/child rates applied where listed):

- Day 1: Krupówki stroll (0 PLN) + Tatra Museum family ticket (59 PLN) + Aqua Park family package, 2 adults + 1 child 3-hr (185 PLN) + 1 extra child ticket at discounted rate (≈35 PLN) ≈ **279 PLN**
- Day 2: Gubałówka (2× 44 PLN adult + 2× 35 PLN child ≈ 158 PLN) + Dolina Strążyska (TNP entrance, modest per-person fee, estimated ≈ 4 × 6 PLN ≈ 24 PLN) + Wielka Krokiew (4 × 12 PLN = 48 PLN) ≈ **230 PLN**
- Day 3: Park Miejski (0 PLN) + Mini Club indoor playground (2 children, estimated ≈ 2 × 25 PLN ≈ 50 PLN) ≈ **50 PLN**

**Trip activities total ≈ 279 + 230 + 50 = 559 PLN** for the family of 4 across all three days.

This is an estimate — several per-person figures (Tatra National Park entrance fee, Mini Club per-child ticket) were not published as exact PLN amounts on the sourced pages and are flagged as assumptions below. Even at the higher end of plausible per-person add-ons, the total should comfortably stay under 700 PLN, leaving the bulk of the remaining ~1550–2550 PLN (after the ≈450 PLN transport cost) for accommodation and food.

## Rationale & Assumptions

- **No Morskie Oko day trip.** Morskie Oko is Zakopane's best-known nature destination, but the walk is 7.9 km one-way (≈2–3.5 hrs each way) — while stroller-passable per family hiking guides, a round trip of 4+ hours of walking plus drive/parking time is not a "relaxed pace appropriate for young children" per the requirements' optional preference, and would eat most of the one full day (13 July) and its budget (Tatra NP parking alone is 35–75 PLN, per `transport.md`). Dolina Strążyska was chosen instead as a genuinely easier, well-documented family/child trail delivering similar Tatra-valley scenery in a fraction of the time and effort, better matching QG7 (kid-suitability) and the budget-conscious framing.
- **Kasprowy Wierch cable car omitted.** At ~1,985 m the summit is frequently in cloud/fog during a rainy period like this trip's forecast (light rain days 1 and 3, drizzle day 2), which would undercut the panoramic-view payoff of a pricier high-mountain cable car; Gubałówka (lower altitude, cheaper, funicular rather than open cable car, hilltop playground) was chosen as the better weather-and-budget fit for a family with a 5- and 8-year-old.
- **Day 1 (arrival) kept intentionally light** given the family arrives after the ~4h55m driving day described in `transport.md`; activities are short, low-exertion, and biased toward indoor/rain-tolerant options (Tatra Museum, Aqua Park) consistent with 12 July's forecast of persistent light rain and cool 11–15°C temperatures in `weather.md`.
- **Day 2 (13 July) is the trip's outdoor-heavy day**, deliberately, because `weather.md` marks it the "driest day of the three" with warming afternoon temperatures (up to 20°C) — the three activities (Gubałówka, Dolina Strążyska, Wielka Krokiew) are all low-exertion/short-duration so they fit comfortably in one day with lunch/rest breaks between them, without exceeding a relaxed pace for young children.
- **Day 3 (departure) kept to two short activities** before the family needs to begin the ~4h55m drive home; both are quick, one free (town park) and one indoor (rain fallback), so the day is not over-scheduled.
- **No duplicate attractions**: each of the eight activities across the three days is a distinct venue (Krupówki, Tatra Museum, Aqua Park, Gubałówka, Dolina Strążyska, Wielka Krokiew, Park Miejski, Mini Club) — QG3 satisfied.
- **Exact per-person costs not published for every attraction** (Tatra National Park entrance fee for Dolina Strążyska; Mini Club's exact per-child ticket price) were not shown as a specific PLN figure on the verified source pages at fetch time; the totals above use conservative, clearly-labelled estimates for those two line items rather than inventing precise official figures. If `budget-aggregator` needs firmer numbers, these two rows are the ones to re-verify.
- **Discount/"concession" ticket categories are assumed to apply to both children (ages 5 and 8)** for Gubałówka and the Tatra Museum, consistent with standard Polish attraction pricing conventions (child/student discount bands), since neither source page broke out an under-10 rate separately from the general "discounted"/"concession" category.
- **Aqua Park family package** (185 PLN for 2 adults + 1 child, 3 hrs) is priced for 3 of the 4 family members; the fourth (second child) is added at the discounted per-person rate (~35 PLN) since no 2-adult+2-child family tier was published on the sourced pricing page.
- **Weather contingency**: every outdoor activity listed (Krupówki, Gubałówka, Dolina Strążyska, Wielka Krokiew, Park Miejski) can be shortened or skipped in heavier rain without losing the day's "≥1 meaningful activity" requirement, since each day also pairs at least one fully indoor option (Tatra Museum + Aqua Park on Day 1; Mini Club on Day 3) or a very short outdoor commitment.
