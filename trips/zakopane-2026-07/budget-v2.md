## Assumptions

- **Accommodation rate commitment:** Hotel Helios family room is committed to the low end of its previously cited 900–1,100 PLN range (450 PLN/night × 2 nights = 900 PLN). This is a deliberate coordinator-directed choice per the iteration plan to restore budget buffer, not a newly-verified market rate. The traveler should confirm actual live pricing via the hotel's booking engine or by phone before finalizing and should be prepared that the true booked rate may be higher (see `accommodation-v2.md` Rationale for the trivago signal).

- **Free hotel parking realized:** The 60 PLN hotel parking cost in `transport.md`'s original total (446.6 PLN) is offset by Hotel Helios's confirmed free on-site parking, per `accommodation-v2.md`. This yields the corrected 386.6 PLN transport subtotal used here.

- **Food baseline assumes milk-bar dinners throughout:** The food total (900 PLN) assumes all three dinners are taken at Regionalny Bar Mleczny (milk bar, ~35 PLN/person full meal). Optional one-off splurge meals at Bistro Schronisko Krupówki or Krupowa Izba are listed in `food-v2.md` as alternatives but not costed into this baseline. If the family chooses to substitute one or more dinners for a sit-down bistro meal, actual spend will be somewhat higher (est. +30–60 PLN/splurge dinner).

- **Transport total assumes own car, not a rental:** Per `transport.md` Rationale, cost is based on fuel + tolls + parking for an owned vehicle; no rental-car daily rate or deposit is included. If the family does not have access to a car, this estimate requires revision.

- **No contingency line item included; real buffer exists in ceiling headroom:** Rather than allocating a separate 5–10% contingency item, the cost rollup is compared directly against the 2,000–3,000 PLN ceiling. The 254.4 PLN gap (8.5%) provides real contingency to absorb variances (accommodation rate, activity add-ons, meals, weather-dependent activity changes) without overshooting the limit.

- **All source artifacts are at the versions specified:** Transport (unchanged from v1), Accommodation (v2, revised low-end commitment), Activities (unchanged from v1), Food (v2, revised milk-bar dinner baseline). No further revisions are assumed.
