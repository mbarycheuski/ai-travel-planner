## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Hard requirement — swimming pool**: Verified via direct `WebFetch` of the hotel's own site (hotel-helios.pl), which explicitly describes a "przestronna strefa relaksu" (spacious relaxation zone) with "basen oraz sauna" (pool and sauna), open daily 12:00–21:00, plus independent confirmation from Hotels.com ("Seasonal outdoor pool") and KAYAK ("indoor pool"). All three sources were fetched/searched live for this run; QG6 satisfied.
- **Family-friendly**: Confirmed 1-/2-/3-/4-person room configurations and a 2-room family apartment, children's playroom, outdoor playground/sandbox, children under 3 free — from the official site and Hotels.com, both directly fetched.
- **Star rating / type**: 2★ budget hotel, consistent with the requirements' assumption of "a budget-to-mid-range hotel consistent with the stated 2000–3000 PLN total trip budget" (no minimum star rating was stated by the traveler, so 2★ with verified amenities is acceptable).
- **Pricing — explicit assumption**: Live per-date dynamic pricing on Booking.com could not be retrieved via `WebFetch` (the booking widget returned no static content — a JS-rendered page, not a dead/fabricated link; the property page itself, hotel-helios.pl, and Hotels.com/KAYAK static listings _were_ successfully fetched and used for the citations above). Cross-referencing (a) KAYAK's cited nightly range of $86–$120 (≈344–480 PLN at ~4.0 PLN/USD) for standard rooms, (b) a third-party reseller's "from 101 PLN" entry-level single-room rate, and (c) the hotel's own site referencing 160–325 PLN/night promotional packages for smaller rooms, a 4-person family room/2-room apartment in peak July season is estimated at **≈450–550 PLN/night** — higher than the smallest single/double rooms cited, since the family needs a 4-person configuration. This is flagged as an **estimate, not a directly-quoted family-room rate**, because exact dated availability pricing requires the interactive booking engine.
- **Alternative properties considered but not selected**: Hotel Czarny Potok Wellness & Spa (indoor pool, wellness centre, but priced ~$88–158/night for standard rooms alone — likely pushes a family room well above the 1,200 PLN ceiling) and Hotel Paryski Art & Business (indoor pool/jacuzzi/sauna, ~387 PLN/night quoted for a standard room) were both found in research but not chosen: Hotel Helios best balances the confirmed pool requirement, family-room availability, free parking (a direct budget saver vs. the transport plan's assumption), and central walkable location, at a comparable or lower price point.
- **Occupancy assumption**: Assumed the family of 4 books a single 4-person family room or 2-room apartment rather than two separate rooms, to keep cost within budget; this is consistent with "family room" language in the requirements' accommodation preference.
- **No accessibility adaptations** were researched or required, per requirements ("Accessibility needs: none").
