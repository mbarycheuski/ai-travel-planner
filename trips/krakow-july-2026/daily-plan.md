## Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1 — Saturday, 18 July — Kraków

- **06:40** — Depart [Łódź Fabryczna by PKP Intercity](https://www.intercity.pl/en/); breakfast onboard.
- **09:05** — Arrive Kraków Główny; walk through the Planty to the Old Town.
- **09:30** — Explore the [Main Market Square (Rynek Główny) & St. Mary's Basilica](https://www.krakow.pl/english/), hear the hourly hejnał trumpet call.
- **11:00** — [Wawel Royal Castle & the Dragon's Den](https://wawel.krakow.pl/en) — courtyards and the kids' favourite dragon cave.
- **13:00** — Lunch at [Pod Wawelem](https://podwawelem.eu/) — hearty Polish plates by the castle.
- **14:30** — [Rynek Underground Museum](https://podziemiarynku.com/en/) — interactive medieval Kraków, great for children.
- **16:30** — Ice cream on the square + [Cloth Hall (Sukiennice) souvenir stalls](https://mnk.pl/branch/the-cloth-hall).
- **18:00** — Early dinner at [Miód Malina](https://miodmalina.pl/).
- **20:05** — Return train to Łódź; arrive ~22:35.

**Note:** With younger kids, the Wawel Dragon's Den closes late afternoon — visit before lunch as scheduled.

## Travel Tips

- **Trumpet call:** Time your square visit for the top of the hour to catch St. Mary's hejnał.
- **Book ahead:** Reserve train seats early — summer Saturdays fill fast.
- **Cash for stalls:** Cloth Hall souvenir vendors and pretzel carts often prefer cash.
- **Beat the heat:** Do Wawel in the late morning; use the shaded Planty park to walk between sights.
