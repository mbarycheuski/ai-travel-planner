# Lisbon Family Trip — August 1–5, 2026

## Trip Summary

- **Destination:** Lisbon, Portugal (single-base city stay; Costa da Caparica as a beach day trip, not a separate stop)
- **Dates:** Saturday, Aug 1 – Wednesday, Aug 5, 2026 (5 days / 4 nights)
- **Party:** 2 adults + 2 children (ages 5 and 8)
- **Transport mode:** Flight (Warsaw ⇄ Lisbon, nonstop), metro/local transit within Lisbon
- **Total estimated cost:** €3,144.33 vs. €3,500.00 budget cap — **€355.67 under budget**
- **Trip shape:** Relaxed, low-exertion, heat-and-pacing-aware sightseeing in central Lisbon, one beach day trip, indoor/air-conditioned activities scheduled on the hottest days, and family-friendly dining throughout

## Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1 — Sat, Aug 1, 2026 — Lisbon (arrival day, 27°C, windy)

- **Transport leg:** [LOT Polish Airlines](https://www.aviation24.be/airlines/lot-polish-airlines/lot-polish-airlines-launches-direct-flights-from-warsaw-to-lisbon/) nonstop, Warsaw Chopin (WAW) → Lisbon Humberto Delgado (LIS), morning departure, ~3h30m flight time; then [Metro Red Line](https://lisbonlisboaportugal.com/lisbon-transport/lisbon-airport-to-city-centre-metro-bus-taxi.html) from the airport to the central hotel area (Baixa/Chiado), ~25–30 min.
- **Activities:**
  - [Lisboa Story Centre](https://www.lisboastorycentre.pt/en/content/pricing-lisboa-story-centre) — indoor, air-conditioned multimedia history exhibit (~1–1.5h)
  - [Praça do Comércio](https://www.visitlisboa.com/en/places/praca-do-comercio-terreiro-do-paco) — riverside square, evening stroll (~45 min), free
- **Dining:** [Time Out Market Lisboa](https://www.timeout.com/time-out-market-lisboa) for dinner (indoor, climate-controlled food hall, good for a jet-lagged arrival evening); optional quick bite at [O Trevo](https://foodlovertour.com/blog/the-bifana-a-lisbon-food-essential/) for a bifana sandwich near Praça do Comércio
- **Overnight:** [NH Collection Lisboa Liberdade — Family Interconnecting Room](https://www.nh-collection.com/en/hotel/nh-collection-lisboa-liberdade)

### Day 2 — Sun, Aug 2, 2026 — Lisbon / Belém (26°C, windy, shaded sightseeing)

- **Transport:** Short travel within Lisbon to the Belém district (local transport per Getting There & Around)
- **Activities:**
  - [Jerónimos Monastery cloisters](https://www.lisboacard.org/tickets/jeronimos-monastery/) — shaded stone cloisters (~1.5h), scheduled late morning to avoid midday heat
  - [MAAT (Museum of Art, Architecture and Technology)](https://www.maat.pt/en/plan-a-visit) — fully air-conditioned, wheelchair-accessible (~1.5–2h)
- **Dining:** Brunch/lunch at [Miolo](https://restaurantguru.com/Miolo-Lisbon-2) (5-minute walk from Jerónimos, vegetarian options available); pastel de nata stop at [Pastéis de Belém](https://pasteisdebelem.pt/en/); dinner at [A Vela](https://ousortiralisbonne.com/en/eating-in-belem-our-5-favorite-spots/), a short walk from MAAT
- **Overnight:** [NH Collection Lisboa Liberdade — Family Interconnecting Room](https://www.nh-collection.com/en/hotel/nh-collection-lisboa-liberdade)

### Day 3 — Mon, Aug 3, 2026 — Costa da Caparica beach day trip (29°C, warming)

- **Transport leg:** [Ferry (Cais do Sodré → Cacilhas) + TST bus (Cacilhas → Costa da Caparica)](https://lisbonlisboaportugal.com/costa-da-caparica/Lisbon-to-costa-caparica-bus.html), round trip
- **Activities:**
  - [Costa da Caparica beach — morning session](https://lisbonlisboaportugal.com/costa-da-caparica/Costa-da-Caparica-portugal.html) (~1.5h, 9:00–10:30am, lifeguarded family beach)
  - Midday shade break at a [beachfront café/promenade](https://lisbonlisboaportugal.com/costa-da-caparica/Costa-da-Caparica-portugal.html) (~3h, 11am–2pm) to honor the strict midday heat-avoidance window
  - [Costa da Caparica beach — late-afternoon session](https://lisbonlisboaportugal.com/costa-da-caparica/Costa-da-Caparica-portugal.html) (~2h, 5:00–7:00pm)
- **Dining:** Lunch at [Restaurante Cabana do Pescador](https://www.cabanadopescador.pt/) (beachfront, family-run since 1974, entertaining parrots/macaws); alternative treat option: [Marisqueira Sotavento](https://www.thefork.com/restaurant/marisqueira-sotavento-r538221)
- **Overnight:** [NH Collection Lisboa Liberdade — Family Interconnecting Room](https://www.nh-collection.com/en/hotel/nh-collection-lisboa-liberdade)

### Day 4 — Tue, Aug 4, 2026 — Lisbon / Parque das Nações (35°C, peak heat, indoor/AC day)

- **Transport:** Local transit to Parque das Nações
- **Activities:**
  - [Oceanário de Lisboa](https://oceanario.pt/en/visit/tickets/) — fully air-conditioned aquarium, largely flat single-level circuit (~2–2.5h)
  - [Pavilhão do Conhecimento (Ciência Viva science museum)](https://www.pavconhecimento.pt/en/visit-us/ticketoffice) — hands-on interactive exhibits for ages 5–8, air-conditioned (~1.5h)
- **Dining:** Lunch at [Nova Peixaria](https://www.tasteoflisboa.com/blog/parque-das-nacoes-travel-guide-for-food-lovers/) (casual fish bar); dinner at [Nova Cervejaria](https://www.novacervejaria.pt/en/), indoor AC dining room, a short walk from the Oceanário/Pavilhão
- **Overnight:** [NH Collection Lisboa Liberdade — Family Interconnecting Room](https://www.nh-collection.com/en/hotel/nh-collection-lisboa-liberdade)

### Day 5 — Wed, Aug 5, 2026 — Lisbon (36°C, peak heat, departure day)

- **Activities:**
  - [Castelo de São Jorge](https://castelodesaojorge.pt/en/information/useful-information/) — early-morning visit (~1.5h, 9:00–10:30am), shaded battlement walkways, before the day's heat peaks
  - Remainder of the day is unscheduled hotel rest/packing time ahead of the airport transfer (no additional bookable attraction)
- **Dining:** Lunch/early dinner at [Pizzaria Lisboa @ Bairro do Avillez](https://www.bairrodoavillez.pt/en/) (indoor dining, short ride from the castle), before heading to the airport
- **Transport leg:** [Metro Red Line](https://lisbonlisboaportugal.com/lisbon-transport/lisbon-airport-to-city-centre-metro-bus-taxi.html) from the central hotel to Lisbon Humberto Delgado Airport (~25–30 min), then [LOT Polish Airlines](https://www.aviation24.be/airlines/lot-polish-airlines/lot-polish-airlines-launches-direct-flights-from-warsaw-to-lisbon/) nonstop return, Lisbon (LIS) → Warsaw Chopin (WAW), afternoon/evening, ~3h30m flight time

## Travel Tips

- The traveler's "minimal walking due to summer heat" is treated as a pacing/comfort preference, not a mobility or disability need — no formal accessibility accommodations were required, though MAAT is noted as wheelchair-accessible.
- No dietary restrictions were stated for the party; all restaurant picks are standard omnivore Portuguese/international menus, with Miolo additionally offering vegetarian options.
- Days 4 and 5 are the hottest of the trip (35–36°C) — indoor/air-conditioned activities are scheduled through the midday hours, with hydration, electrolytes, and high-SPF sunscreen recommended throughout.
- The Day 3 beach outing is split into a morning and a late-afternoon session bracketing a shaded midday rest, to avoid the 11am–4pm heat window.
- Jerónimos Monastery and Castelo de São Jorge offer free entry for children up to age 12, so both children (ages 5 and 8) enter free at these venues.
- All costs throughout are quoted in EUR, the confirmed trip currency — no currency conversion required.
- Flight fares, the nightly hotel rate, and several restaurant per-person prices are planning-stage estimates and should be re-confirmed closer to booking.
