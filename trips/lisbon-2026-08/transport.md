## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Airline/route choice**: LOT Polish Airlines operates a daily nonstop Warsaw–Lisbon service (Boeing 737 MAX 8), which was prioritized over Ryanair's Warsaw-Modlin routing and any 1-stop TAP/Wizz Air itinerary because it departs from Warsaw Chopin (closer to most Warsaw origins than Modlin) and keeps both the outbound and return each to a single ~3.5-hour nonstop leg — consistent with the requirement's minimal-legs/pacing assumption, which explicitly treats the Warsaw–Lisbon flight as the one unavoidable long leg. Source: [Aviation24 — LOT launches Warsaw–Lisbon route](https://www.aviation24.be/airlines/lot-polish-airlines/lot-polish-airlines-launches-direct-flights-from-warsaw-to-lisbon/).
- **Fare estimate (assumption)**: A live fare search (eDreams, LOT route) showed a cheapest-72-hours fare of €93 and typical sample fares of €131–€287 round trip on this route (non-peak dates). August 1–5 falls in EU summer peak season with two young children, so €200 per person round trip is assumed as a representative August fare — higher than the cited off-peak floor, within the cited sample range's upper half. This is an assumption, not a confirmed August 2026 quote; exact pricing should be re-checked closer to booking. Children are assumed to require full-fare seats (no infant discount applies at ages 5 and 8).
- **Specific flight numbers**: Route/schedule sources (Aviation24, flight-data aggregators) confirm the daily nonstop LOT service and its ~3h30m flight time, but do not guarantee a specific flight number will still operate exactly as scheduled by August 2026; "representative departure time" is noted as an assumption pending final booking, not a locked reservation.
- **Currency**: All costs quoted in EUR (Lisbon-tourism-guide sources already list Portugal transit/taxi fares natively in EUR; the LOT fare data was in EUR/PLN mixed sources — EUR figures were used directly, no conversion applied since EUR is already the trip currency per `requirements.md`).
- **Hotel location assumption**: Local transport legs (Legs 2–3) assume the central hotel sits in the Baixa/Chiado corridor, consistent with the accommodation preference "central hotel, family room" and the requirements' assumption of a 3–4 star central-area hotel; if the confirmed hotel sits elsewhere (e.g., Avenida/Marquês de Pombal), the same Red Line metro connection still applies with a short walk adjustment.
- **Day-trip beaches**: Cascais and Costa da Caparica are used as the two candidate beach day trips per the requirements' explicit (unconfirmed) suggestion; local-transport costs for both are included so `activities-planner`/`budget-aggregator` can select either or both without re-costing transport.
- **Navegante pass usage assumption**: The trip total assumes 2 general sightseeing days on the standard 24h pass, 1 Cascais day on the CP-inclusive pass, and 1 Costa da Caparica day via ferry+bus single tickets (cheaper for a one-off crossing than upgrading the whole day's pass) — actual day allocation is set by `activities-planner`'s itinerary and may shift which days use which pass.
- **Taxi/Uber contingency**: Included as a heat/pacing safety margin (per the requirements' explicit minimal-walking/heat constraint) for two short trips where a family with young children may prefer a direct car over a transfer-heavy public-transit route; actual usage is optional and could reduce the total if unused.
- **Warsaw-side airport transfer**: Not included — out of scope, as the family departs from their own Warsaw location to Warsaw Chopin Airport by means not specified in requirements (domestic, non-Lisbon transport).
