## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Property choice**: NH Collection Lisboa Liberdade was selected over other central candidates because it is the only property found that combines (a) a single bookable family room fitting 2 adults + 2 children (the 40 m² Family Interconnecting Room, not two separate rooms to book/pay for), (b) a rooftop pool open through August — directly addressing the heat/pacing constraint in `requirements.md` (minimal walking, midday relief for young children), (c) full air conditioning, and (d) a 4-star rating on/immediately off Avenida da Liberdade, one metro stop from the Baixa/Chiado core that `transport.md` assumed for its transfer costing. Source pages verified live via `WebFetch`: [hotel page](https://www.nh-collection.com/en/hotel/nh-collection-lisboa-liberdade), [rooms page](https://www.nh-collection.com/en/hotel/nh-collection-lisboa-liberdade/rooms).
- **Properties considered and rejected**:
  - *AlmaLusa Baixa/Chiado* (right in Baixa/Chiado, 4-star boutique, AC) — its Triple Room caps at 3 guests (2 adults + 1 child), so a family of 4 would need either two separate rooms or the 133 m² Top Floor Apartment, both materially over the ~€1,000–1,500 target; also has no pool. Source: [AlmaLusa hotel rooms page](https://www.almalusahotels.com/baixachiado/hotel-rooms).
  - *Hotel Lisboa Plaza* (4-star, family-owned, just off Avenida da Liberdade, family room for up to 4 with AC) — good budget fit but has no pool, which the heat/pacing constraint marks as a preferred (not mandatory) feature; kept as a fallback if the NH Collection family room is unavailable for these dates. Source: [Lisbon Heritage Hotels — Lisbon Plaza Hotel](https://lisbonheritagehotels.com/lisbon-plaza-hotel/).
  - *Hotel do Chiado* (4-star, prime Chiado location, AC, private terraces) — no family room / no rooms configured for more than 2 guests; would require booking two rooms, pushing cost over budget. Source: [Trivago listing](https://www.trivago.com/en-US/oar/hotel-do-chiado-lisbon?search=100-34762).
- **Rate estimate (explicit assumption)**: Booking.com and Tripadvisor pages for this hotel returned dynamic/JS-rendered content that could not be scraped for a live August 1–5, 2026 quote via `WebFetch` (empty body / 403 responses). The €320/night figure for the Family Interconnecting Room is therefore an estimate built from cited comparison points that *were* retrievable: Hotels.com listed a standard double from **$226/night** (~€208 at an assumed $1 ≈ €0.92 rate) and Kayak showed recent booked-rate samples of **$307–$332/night** (~€282–€305) for the same hotel, both presumably reflecting a mix of dates. €320/night applies (a) the higher end of that range as an August-peak-season baseline and (b) a modest premium for the larger Family Interconnecting Room over a standard double. This should be re-verified with a live quote before booking. Source data: [Hotels.com listing](https://www.hotels.com/ho230421/nh-collection-lisboa-liberdade-lisbon-portugal/), [Kayak listing](https://www.kayak.com/Lisbon-Hotels-NH-Collection-Lisboa-Liberdade.20790.ksp).
- **Currency**: All figures quoted directly in EUR, the confirmed trip currency (`requirements.md`). The one USD-denominated source (Hotels.com) was converted at an assumed $1 ≈ €0.92 for comparison purposes only; the €320/night figure used in the total is a EUR-native estimate, not a direct conversion.
- **Pool seasonality assumption**: The NH Collection's rooftop pool is described across sources as seasonal/outdoor; August falls within its typical operating season (spring–early autumn), consistent with heat-relief value for the stay, but exact 2026 operating dates were not separately confirmed and should be checked closer to the trip.
- **Parking/accessibility**: No parking need was assumed (family travels by flight + metro per `transport.md`, no rental car); the hotel does offer paid parking (€19/day) if that changes. No accessibility/mobility need was stated in `requirements.md`, so none was screened for beyond standard family-room capacity.
- **Breakfast/meals**: Room rate assumed room-only/standard rate; breakfast cost (if any) is left to the `budget-aggregator`/food-planning agent rather than folded into this accommodation subtotal, consistent with keeping this artifact scoped to lodging only.
