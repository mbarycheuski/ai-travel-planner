## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Beach destination choice**: Both Cascais and Costa da Caparica were pre-costed in `transport.md`. Costa da Caparica was selected as the single beach day trip because its ferry+bus route is shorter and involves less transfer-point sun exposure than the Cascais suburban-rail option, and its northern beaches (e.g. Praia de São João) are explicitly lifeguarded and family-oriented. Cascais was not used, to avoid an unnecessary second beach day and keep pacing light per the heat/walking constraint.
- **Beach day scheduled on Day 3, not Days 4–5**: Day 3 (29°C, moderate wind, "warming trend") is the mildest of the remaining days; Days 4–5 (35–36°C, per `weather.md`) are reserved for indoor/AC attractions as instructed. Beach time on Day 3 is split into an early-morning session (before 11am) and a late-afternoon/evening session (after 4pm), with a shaded/indoor midday rest in between — directly honoring the "strictly limit midday walking 11am–4pm" note in `weather.md`.
- **Lisboa Story Centre child price**: The official pricing page's numeric tables are rendered as images not extractable via fetch; a half-adult-price child rate (~€3.50) is assumed based on typical Lisbon attraction child-discount patterns and one review citing a €7 adult rate. Flagged for re-confirmation at booking.
- **Oceanário / Pavilhão do Conhecimento age brackets**: Both children (ages 5 and 8) fall within the venues' stated child tiers (Oceanário 3–12; Pavilhão 3–17), so both qualify for child/family rates as costed.
- **Jerónimos Monastery and Castelo de São Jorge child admission**: Both venues state free entry for children up to age 12, so both children (5 and 8) are assumed to enter free; no child-ticket line item was added for these two rows.
- **Pavilhão do Conhecimento family package**: Costed using the venue's published "family package" (2 adults + children under 17) at €35 flat, cheaper than summing individual adult/child tickets (€15 × 2 + €10 × 2 = €50).
- **No currency conversion needed**: All source prices were already quoted in EUR (Portugal's currency, matching the trip currency confirmed in `requirements.md`); no conversion was required for any row.
- **Costa da Caparica beach itself has no entry fee**: Only the transport to/from it (already itemized in `transport.md`'s Local Transport section) has a cost; an optional beach umbrella/lounger rental (~€10–15) is noted but not included in the activities total since it's discretionary and not confirmed as needed.
- **Day 5 pacing**: Given the 36°C peak-heat forecast and an afternoon/evening return flight, only one short (~1.5h) outdoor activity is scheduled, deliberately placed in the early morning per `weather.md`'s own suggestion ("São Jorge Castle grounds early morning"), followed by air-conditioned hotel rest before the airport transfer — consistent with the mandatory midday indoor-rest guidance for this day.
- **Day 5 "Hotel rest / pack, then airport transfer" removed from the recommendation table**: This item is pure logistics (no attraction, no bookable experience, no cost beyond what `transport.md` already itemizes for the airport transfer), so it has no real source page to cite and does not belong in a `## Activities by Day/Stop` recommendation row. It is instead described in prose immediately after the Day 5 table above. This does not change the Day 5 or grand total, since the item was always costed at €0.00.
- **No accessibility needs assumed**: Per `requirements.md`, the family's "minimal walking" preference is a comfort/pacing preference, not a mobility/disability accommodation; suitability notes above address family/child comfort (shade, AC, low walking distance) rather than formal accessibility (step-free, mobility-aid) requirements, though MAAT's stated wheelchair accessibility is noted where the source mentioned it.
- **No duplicate attractions**: Each of the eight scheduled items (Lisboa Story Centre, Praça do Comércio, Jerónimos Monastery, MAAT, Costa da Caparica beach, Oceanário de Lisboa, Pavilhão do Conhecimento, Castelo de São Jorge) appears exactly once across the itinerary.
- **Every day has a kid-appropriate item and ≥1 meaningful activity**: confirmed day-by-day above (Story Centre/square; Jerónimos/MAAT; beach sessions; aquarium/science museum; castle) — all explicitly family/child-suitable per QG8.
