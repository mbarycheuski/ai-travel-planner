1. **Minor cuts to food** (−15–25 PLN): select the absolute lowest-cost meal option at one venue (e.g., a smaller plate at breakfast, skip the optional fish snack), reducing the 430 PLN food estimate to ~405–415 PLN.

3. **No contingency buffer**: Scenario A assumes no contingency; if a buffer is required, the trip approaches 1,388–1,454 PLN depending on the margin selected.

**Recommendation:**

- **If 1,300 PLN is a firm ceiling**: proceed with Scenario A (low-cost food selections, no contingency). This is feasible and brings the trip to **1,322 PLN** — just 22 PLN (1.7%) over, absorb-able through careful meal choices or by treating the contingency as "already allocated" to absorption of minor overspends.
- **If a 5–10% contingency is mandatory**: recommend a cap increase to **1,350–1,400 PLN** to safely accommodate realistic meal costs and a modest risk buffer. Scenario A + 5% contingency = 1,388 PLN; Scenario A + 10% = 1,454 PLN is the risk-adjusted range.
- **If cost must stay at 1,300 PLN or below**: the margin is thin (22 PLN); prioritize the absolute lowest meal options, and flag that any unplanned expense (e.g., a parking surcharge, a small activity entrance upgrade, or a miscalculated fuel price variance) would breach the cap.

- **Trip scope**: 2 adults, 1 night stay (Friday evening ~21:30–22:00 arrival through Saturday evening ~18:00–19:00 departure), per `requirements-v2.md`.

- **Currency**: All costs are quoted in PLN (Poland's trip currency) per `requirements-v2.md`. No currency conversion performed; all source artifacts cite PLN natively.

- **Budget cap revision**: The limit has been updated from 1,000 PLN (budget.md) to 1,300 PLN (`requirements-v2.md`). This revised `budget-v2.md` reflects the new cap; Scenario A now falls only 22 PLN over the new limit, whereas it was 322 PLN over the original 1,000 PLN cap.

- **Accommodation recommendation**: The recommended option (**ibis Gdańsk Stare Miasto**, 271 PLN including on-site parking) is used in the primary totals. **Budget alternative** (Moon Hostel + Interparking fallback, ~179 PLN) and **upscale alternative** (B&B Hotel Gdańsk Old Town, ~408 PLN) are available per `accommodation.md`. The accommodation cost in the table shows the recommended mid-range choice; totals should be recalculated if a different tier is selected.

  - **Low (430 PLN)**: Mandu pierogi bar for Friday dinner, milk bar (Bar Mleczny Neptun) for Saturday lunch, and Mandu again for Saturday dinner (budget option); Pomelo bistro for Saturday breakfast (no cheaper option researched).

  - An even lower tier (~320 PLN) is theoretically possible only if Pomelo breakfast is substituted with a cheaper option, but no such option was verified in `food.md`.
