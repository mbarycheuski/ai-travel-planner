# Gdańsk Weekend Getaway — 17–18 July 2026

## Trip Summary

- **Destination:** Gdańsk, Poland
- **Origin:** Łódź, Poland
- **Dates:** Depart Friday, 17 July 2026 (after 18:00) — return Saturday, 18 July 2026 (evening)
- **Duration:** 2 calendar days, 1 night
- **Travelers:** 2 adults
- **Transport mode:** Car (own vehicle, round trip)
- **Budget:** 1,230 PLN estimated vs. 1,300 PLN cap — **PASS**, ~70 PLN under cap (~5% margin)

## Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1 — Friday, 17 July 2026 — Łódź → Gdańsk

- **Transport leg:** Łódź → Gdańsk via A1/S6 expressway (through Włocławek, Grudziądz), 339 km, ~3h 25min, departing after 18:00. Fuel + toll ≈ 183 PLN. [Route: Łódź–Gdańsk (conadrogach.pl)](https://conadrogach.pl/wyznaczanie-trasy/lodz-gdansk/)
- **Arrival:** ~21:30–22:00, expect car parked at Interparking Centrum Gdańsk (see *Getting There & Around*).
- **Check-in:** [Moon Hostel Gdańsk](https://moonhostel.pl/pl/gdansk) (private double room, private bath) — 24h reception comfortably supports the late arrival.
- **Evening activity (free, ~1h15–1h45 total):**
  - Evening walk: Długi Targ (Long Market) & Neptune's Fountain, floodlit Old Town, 45–60 min. [Długi Targ — In Your Pocket](https://www.inyourpocket.com/gdansk/dlugi-targ_16178v)
  - Motława River Embankment (Długie Pobrzeże) night stroll, views of the Crane, 30–45 min. Dress in layers — light rain risk ~13%. [Długie Pobrzeże / Long Waterfront — In Your Pocket](https://www.inyourpocket.com/gdansk/long-waterfront_163640v)
- **Dinner:** Pierogarnia Mandu (Centrum, ul. Elżbietańska 4/8), kitchen open to 22:00, ~45–55 PLN/person. Vegan/vegetarian pierogi options available. [Menu — Pierogarnia Mandu Gdańsk Centrum](https://pierogarnia-mandu.pl/en/gdansk-centrum/menu/)
  - Backup if closed/full: Restauracja Kubicki (ul. Wartka 5), open to 23:00. [Restauracja Kubicki — Menu](https://restauracjakubicki.pl/index.php/en/menu)

### Day 2 — Saturday, 18 July 2026 — Gdańsk → Łódź

Weather-flexible — do the climb if morning is dry. [St Mary's Basilica — official visitor info](https://bazylikamariacka.gdansk.pl/bazylika/informacja-dla-turystow/)

[Milk Bars in Gdańsk — In Your Pocket](https://www.inyourpocket.com/gdansk/milk-bars-in-gdansk-sopot-gdynia_70657f)

- **Afternoon:** Motława River Embankment (Długie Pobrzeże) daytime walk + Gdańsk Crane exterior view, 45–60 min, free. Best in a drier window — umbrella recommended (26% shower risk). [Długie Pobrzeże / Long Waterfront — In Your Pocket](https://www.inyourpocket.com/gdansk/long-waterfront_163640v)
- **Dinner (before evening departure):** Restauracja Kubicki (ul. Wartka 5), open 12:00–23:00, fish/meat mains, ≈100–130 PLN/person for a starter + main. [Restauracja Kubicki — Menu](https://restauracjakubicki.pl/index.php/en/menu)
  - Budget alternative: Pierogarnia Mandu again, ~45–55 PLN/person. [Menu — Pierogarnia Mandu Gdańsk Centrum](https://pierogarnia-mandu.pl/en/gdansk-centrum/menu/)
- **Transport leg:** Gdańsk → Łódź via the same A1/S6 route, 339 km, ~3h 25min, departing evening (~18:00–19:00). Fuel + toll ≈ 183 PLN. [Route: Gdańsk–Łódź (conadrogach.pl)](https://conadrogach.pl/wyznaczanie-trasy/gdansk-lodz/)

## Travel Tips

- **Currency:** PLN — ATMs widely available in Gdańsk; keep some small cash for parking meters/SPP zone.
- **No visa/ID concerns:** domestic Poland trip — ID cards suffice, no passport needed.
- **Dietary:** no dietary constraints stated by travelers; every recommended venue nonetheless has at least one vegetarian option.
- **Accessibility:** no accessibility needs stated; note the St. Mary's tower climb (409 steps, narrow spiral stairs) is strenuous and not accessibility-friendly — the nave (free) is a step-free alternative, and the Amber Museum and European Solidarity Centre serve as accessible, fully indoor options (ECS has a lift).
- **Weather flexibility:** Saturday's 26% shower chance means the Amber Museum and European Solidarity Centre double as rain contingencies; do the St. Mary's tower climb and outdoor waterfront walks in drier windows (morning/early evening).
- **Pacing:** doing all four paid Saturday activities plus a full lunch is a full, tightly-paced day given the evening departure — travelers preferring a lighter pace can drop either the Amber Museum or the European Solidarity Centre without affecting the rest of the plan.
- **Parking confirmation:** Moon Hostel's own on-site parking must be pre-booked by phone (+48 500 331 110) and its price is unpublished; this plan defaults to the Interparking Centrum Gdańsk fallback already reflected in the budget above.
