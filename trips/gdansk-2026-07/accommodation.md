## Recommended pick

**ibis Gdańsk Stare Miasto** is the recommended choice: it sits squarely inside the 150–250 PLN/night target, is a recognized international budget chain with guaranteed on-site secure parking (no advance-reservation uncertainty, unlike the hostel), a 5-minute walk from the Old Town, and standard late check-in support (front desk staffed to handle a post-21:00 Friday arrival) with checkout by 12:00 noon Saturday — comfortably ahead of the evening return drive.

## Parking Details

- **ibis Gdańsk Stare Miasto (recommended)**: on-site, secure, monitored private parking, reported at **~30 PLN/day** ([source](https://www.kayak.com/Gdansk-Hotels-ibis-Gdansk-Stare-Miasto.2595208.ksp) and hotel guest reviews); no pre-booking required, so it comfortably accommodates a Friday ~21:30 arrival and a Saturday daytime departure.
- **Moon Hostel**: parking exists but **must be reserved in advance** and no published price was found; given the trip's late (~21:30) Friday arrival, this is a risk if not pre-arranged by phone (+48 500 331 110). Fallback: use the **Interparking Centrum Gdańsk** garage already priced in `transport.md` (≈60 PLN for the ~20h Fri evening–Sat evening stay), which is a few minutes' walk from all three properties.
- **B&B Hotel Gdańsk Old Town**: private on-site parking at **100 PLN/day** — most expensive parking of the three, consistent with its higher room rate and prime Long Market location.
- All three properties are within Gdańsk's Old Town / Śródmieście paid-parking zone (Interparking Centrum Gdańsk garage referenced in `transport.md` is a viable universal fallback for any of them if hotel parking is full or unavailable at a late Friday arrival).

## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Currency conversion**: ibis and B&B Hotel rates were quoted in USD by the sources consulted ($61 and $78 respectively for representative nightly rates). Converted at an assumed **~3.95 PLN/USD** rate (approximate mid-2026 market rate; not fetched from a live FX source) to 241 PLN and 308 PLN. This is an explicit assumption — actual booking-site PLN pricing on the exact dates (17–18 Jul 2026) may differ; Moon Hostel's 119 PLN rate was quoted natively in PLN, so no conversion was applied there.
- **Live date-specific pricing not directly retrievable**: Booking.com's own hotel pages returned blank/JS-rendered content when fetched directly (likely bot protection), so pricing was corroborated via the properties' official sites (Accor, B&B Hotels) and third-party aggregators (Kayak, Trip.com) that displayed representative/"from" rates rather than a locked-in quote for the exact 17–18 Jul 2026 night. Treat the 241 PLN (ibis) and 308 PLN (B&B) figures as representative estimates to reconfirm at time of booking, not guaranteed rates.
- **Star rating ambiguity**: ibis Gdańsk Stare Miasto was described as "2 stars" by one source and "3-star" by others (typical for the international ibis economy/midscale brand); presented here as 2★ per the official Accor-adjacent listing, with the caveat noted in the table.
- **No stated traveler preferences on stars/parking/family/accessibility**: per `requirements.md`, no specific accommodation preferences were given by the traveler. This plan assumes standard car-parking access is required (car travel mode) and selects a budget-to-mid-range 2–3★ option as a reasonable default, consistent with the assumption already recorded in `requirements.md`. No family-room or accessibility requirement was stated, so none was strictly filtered on, though family/twin configurations and lift access are noted where available as a courtesy.
- **Late Friday arrival / early-ish Saturday departure**: all three properties support standard hotel-style check-in (after 15:00) well ahead of the ~21:30 Friday arrival, and checkout (11:00–12:00) Saturday comfortably precedes the evening return drive; the car can remain parked past checkout if needed (verify with the property directly, as no explicit "late checkout with parked car" policy was found in the sources).
- **Parking price for Moon Hostel not published**: flagged explicitly above; the Interparking Centrum Gdańsk fallback already costed in `transport.md` (≈60 PLN) is the safer planning assumption if this option is chosen.
