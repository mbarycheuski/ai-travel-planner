## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Currency**: all prices are quoted directly in PLN from the sourced menus/pages — the trip currency confirmed in `requirements.md` — so no conversion was needed.
- **Budget conflict flagged**: the launch brief's stated food-budget slice (~80–150 PLN for the whole trip) is not achievable for 2 adults across four meals at real, currently-listed Gdańsk prices, even using the cheapest verified option (milk bar) for every meal — a milk-bar-only plan for 2 adults across 4 meals still runs roughly 2 × (25+25+25+25) ≈ 200 PLN minimum, and a more realistic mixed plan (one bistro breakfast, one sit-down dinner) runs 430–585 PLN for the party. This is flagged for the traveler/orchestrator to reconcile against the overall 1,000 PLN cap (transport.md already books ~427 PLN, leaving ~573 PLN for accommodation + food + activities — the two budget-alternative meals above bring food into the lower end of that remaining envelope, but accommodation and activities must then be trimmed accordingly).
- **Friday dinner timing**: transport.md assumes arrival in Gdańsk ~21:30–22:00. Milk bars (the cheapest category) close by 18:00–20:00 even in season, so they are not usable for Friday dinner; Pierogarnia Mandu Gdańsk Centrum (kitchen open to 22:00) and Restauracja Kubicki (open to 23:00/midnight) are the two verified options that can still serve at that hour.
- **Saturday dinner timing**: "before evening departure" (car leaves Saturday evening per transport.md) — Kubicki's 12:00–23:00 hours comfortably cover an early-to-mid evening meal; Mandu (open to 22:00) is offered as the lower-cost alternative if the higher Kubicki price is out of budget.
- **Dietary constraints**: `requirements.md` states none. Every recommended venue nonetheless has at least one vegetarian or vegetable-based item on its menu (noted per row) as a courtesy buffer, though no formal vegetarian/vegan requirement was confirmed.
- **Milk bar seasonal hours**: Bar Mleczny Neptun's July (in-season) hours (~10:00–19:00 weekends) were used since the visit falls in mid-July; if hours have shifted since the cited source, arrive before 18:00 to be safe.
- **Price ranges are per-person, food/drink only** — they exclude alcohol beyond any bundled item, tips, or the Interparking/toll/fuel costs already covered in `transport.md`.
