## Recommended pick

**Moon Hostel Gdańsk (private double room, private bath)** is now the primary recommendation, swapped in from the prior `accommodation.md` version to close a 22 PLN budget gap flagged by the budget validation (1,322 PLN with ibis vs. the 1,300 PLN cap). At 119 PLN/night, it is 122 PLN cheaper than ibis's 241 PLN room rate. Combined with the ~60 PLN Interparking Centrum Gdańsk fallback (used in place of Moon Hostel's unpriced, pre-booking-only on-site parking), the accommodation + parking line drops from 271 PLN (ibis) to **~179 PLN**, bringing the whole-trip total to **~1,230 PLN** — within the 1,300 PLN cap, with roughly a 5% (~70 PLN) contingency buffer remaining.

- **Location**: ul. Długie Ogrody 6A, ~2-minute walk to the Green Gate and Old Town — fully walkable for this short 1-night, sightseeing-focused trip; no compromise on location versus ibis's ~5-minute walk.
- **Check-in / check-out**: standard hostel-style check-in (front desk staffed 24h, so the ~21:30 Friday arrival is comfortably supported); checkout well ahead of the Saturday evening return drive (reconfirm exact checkout time with the property directly, as no published checkout hour was found — see Rationale).
- **Amenities**: free WiFi, guest kitchen access, private double room with private bathroom (not a shared dorm) — appropriate for 2 adults wanting privacy at budget cost.
- **Contact**: +48 500 331 110 (used to pre-arrange parking if the traveler prefers to skip the Interparking fallback).

## Parking Details

- **Moon Hostel (recommended pick)**: on-site parking exists but **must be reserved in advance by phone** (+48 500 331 110) and no published price was found in available sources. Given the late (~21:30 Friday) arrival, this plan does **not** rely on unconfirmed hostel parking and instead defaults to the **Interparking Centrum Gdańsk** garage (already priced in `transport.md`), at **~60 PLN for the ~20h Fri evening–Sat evening stay**, a few minutes' walk from the hostel. If the traveler pre-books hostel parking directly and it turns out cheaper than 60 PLN, the total could drop further — this is a possible additional saving, not counted in the total below.
- **ibis Gdańsk Stare Miasto (upscale alternative)**: on-site, secure, monitored private parking, reported at **~30 PLN/day** ([source](https://www.kayak.com/Gdansk-Hotels-ibis-Gdansk-Stare-Miasto.2595208.ksp)); no pre-booking required.
- **B&B Hotel Gdańsk Old Town (splurge option)**: private on-site parking at **100 PLN/day** — most expensive parking of the three, consistent with its higher room rate.
- All properties sit within Gdańsk's Old Town / Śródmieście area; the Interparking Centrum Gdańsk garage is a viable universal fallback for any of them if a hotel's own parking is full, unpriced, or unavailable at a late Friday arrival.
