## Assumptions

- **Trip scope**: 2 adults, 1 night stay (Friday evening ~21:30–22:00 arrival through Saturday evening ~18:00–19:00 departure), per `requirements.md`.

- **Currency**: All costs are quoted in PLN (Poland's trip currency) per `requirements.md`. No currency conversion performed; all source artifacts cite PLN natively.

- **Accommodation recommendation**: The recommended option (**ibis Gdańsk Stare Miasto**, 271 PLN including on-site parking) is used in the primary totals. **Budget alternative** (Moon Hostel + Interparking fallback, ~179 PLN) and **upscale alternative** (B&B Hotel Gdańsk Old Town, ~408 PLN) are available per `accommodation.md`. The accommodation cost in the table shows the recommended mid-range choice; totals should be recalculated if a different tier is selected.

- **Food cost tiers**: The 430 PLN "low" and 560 PLN "high" figures represent the range between:
  - **Low (430 PLN)**: Mandu pierogi bar for Friday dinner, milk bar (Bar Mleczny Neptun) for Saturday lunch, and Mandu again for Saturday dinner (budget option); Pomelo bistro for Saturday breakfast (no cheaper option researched).
  - **High (560 PLN)**: Same Friday dinner and breakfast, but Kubicki sit-down restaurant for Saturday dinner (midscale option). Milk bar lunch held constant.
  - An even lower tier (~320 PLN) is theoretically possible only if Pomelo breakfast is substituted with a cheaper option, but no such option was verified in `food.md`.

- **Activities as listed**: 194 PLN covers Saturday paid admissions only:
  - St. Mary's Basilica tower climb: 40 PLN (nave is free)
  - Amber Museum: 74 PLN
  - European Solidarity Centre: 80 PLN
  - Friday evening walk (Długi Targ, Motława Embankment): 0 PLN (free)
  - Saturday daytime Motława walk: 0 PLN (free)
  - Saturday lunch (~40–60 PLN from `activities.md` meal estimate, ~50–70 PLN from `food.md` itemized meal costs) is **not** included in the 194 PLN Activities subtotal to avoid double-counting; it is grouped under Food & Dining totals above.

- **Pacing note**: All four paid attractions in a single day (Saturday, 09:00–18:00) is tight. Realistic plan: skip one attraction (e.g., Amber Museum or ECS) to allow a more relaxed pace or weather contingency, per the pacing note in `activities.md`. Dropping one 74–80 PLN attraction saves ~74–80 PLN but does not fully close the budget gap.

- **Contingency rate**: 5–10% buffer is suggested as a safety margin; this is **not** a mandatory cost per `requirements.md` (which states only the 1,000 PLN trip total), but is presented to show the full risk-adjusted cost if contingency is planned.

- **Budget conflict flagged**: Even the most aggressive cost-cutting scenario (budget accommodation, budget food, skip one activity) yields ~1,050–1,100 PLN, exceeding the 1,000 PLN cap by 50–100 PLN. The transport cost alone (427 PLN) plus a mid-range accommodation (179–271 PLN) consumes 60–80% of the budget before activities and food. The gap is not due to any individual line item being uncompetitive, but rather the sum of realistic costs for 2 adults over a 2-day trip in a city destination. **Recommendation**: Discuss with the traveler whether the 1,000 PLN cap is fixed or can be increased to 1,200–1,300 PLN to accommodate the planned itinerary; if the cap is fixed, rank priorities (transport/accommodation vs. activities vs. dining quality) to understand where cuts should fall.

- **Food cost note from `food.md`**: The artifact notes a stated "budget-food-only" scenario of 250–320 PLN "for the whole trip", but the detailed meal breakdown suggests this figure may be incomplete or reflect only a subset of meals. The 430 PLN low-tier figure (used in the main table) is derived from the explicit meal-by-meal table and is considered the verified minimum.

- **Parking detail**: Transport cost assumes Interparking Centrum Gdańsk garage (~60 PLN for ~20 hours Fri evening to Sat evening). Accommodation cost assumes on-site parking at ibis (30 PLN/day). If the recommended ibis is booked, its on-site parking should be used; the Interparking cost in the transport row is a fallback only.
