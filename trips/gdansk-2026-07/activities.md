## Rationale & Assumptions

- **Pacing reality check**: doing all four paid Saturday attractions (St. Mary's tower, Amber Museum, ECS, plus the free waterfront walk) back-to-back is tight for a single day that also needs a ~18:00–19:00 car departure (per `transport.md`). Recommended realistic itinerary: morning (09:30–12:00) Old Town free walk + St. Mary's tower while dry; late morning/early afternoon (12:00–14:00) Amber Museum; lunch (14:00–15:00) at Długi Targ; afternoon (15:00–17:00) European Solidarity Centre (ECS is the priority indoor pick if showers intensify — has the longest, most weather-proof duration); early evening (17:00–18:00) final Motława riverside walk back toward the parking garage before the 18:00–19:00 departure noted in `transport.md`. If travelers prefer a lighter pace, drop either the Amber Museum or ECS — both are independently swappable without affecting the rest of the plan.
- **Weather adaptation** (per `weather.md`): Saturday carries a 26% chance of scattered showers (4.05 mm) with UV 4.7 at midday. Two of the four attractions (Amber Museum, ECS) are fully indoor and serve as the rain contingency; the tower climb and river walk are the outdoor-exposed items and are scheduled for the morning/early-evening windows where the forecast implications note conditions are generally manageable with an umbrella/rain shell rather than heavy rain.
- **No duplicate attractions**: each of the 5 Saturday rows and 2 Friday rows names a distinct place; the Motława Embankment appears once each on Friday (night) and Saturday (day) deliberately as a different experience (night vs. day), not a duplicate booking/ticket.
- **Currency**: all costs are quoted directly in PLN (the trip currency confirmed in `requirements.md`); no conversion was needed as all official sources (bazylikamariacka.gdansk.pl, muzeumgdansk.pl, bilety.ecs.gda.pl) quote PLN natively.
- **Discount/reduced tickets not applied**: both travelers are adults with no stated student/senior status, so all costs use the "regular/normal" adult ticket price, not the discounted "ulgowy" rate.
- **Lunch cost**: no specific restaurant was verified/linked as a paid recommendation (per QG-CITE, only real bookable/visitable attractions are linked); the 40–60 PLN estimate is a placeholder based on typical budget café pricing in Gdańsk Old Town (two light lunches + drinks), anchored to the same Długi Targ location already linked above rather than a new unverified venue.
- **St. Mary's tower vs. nave-only fallback**: if Saturday morning brings the heavier end of the forecast shower risk, travelers can skip the 409-step tower climb (which requires dry, non-slippery stone stairs) and visit the free nave only, then reallocate the saved 40 PLN and ~30 minutes to a longer ECS visit.
- **Not included / considered and dropped**: Oliwa Cathedral and Westerplatte were considered per the launch-prompt suggestions but dropped — both require a taxi/tram trip outside the walkable Old Town core (10–20 min each way), which does not fit the tight single full-day Saturday window alongside the four attractions above without cutting something else; flagged here as an option if travelers prefer fewer central attractions and more suburban sightseeing instead.
