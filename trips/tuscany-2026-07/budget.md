## Estimated Total

**≈ €2,171** for the 5-day, 4-person Tuscany loop (Florence → Greve in Chianti →
Siena → Pienza → San Gimignano → Florence), including rental car, fuel, tolls,
local parking, accommodation, food, and activities, plus a 10% contingency.

## Against Limit

- Budget cap (from `requirements.md`): **€2,500**, explicitly including rental car + fuel, excluding flights.
- Estimated total: **€2,171**.
- **Result: UNDER by €329** (≈13% headroom below the cap).

Even using the conservative upper-bound transport estimate and a full 10%
contingency, the trip stays under the €2,500 cap. If the low-end transport
figures from `transport.md` are used instead (€465–500 rental+fuel, no parking
line item), the total would be lower still (≈€2,090–2,125), widening the
margin under the limit.

## Gaps / Flags (explicit — not invented, called out per source)

- `transport.md` gives a rental+fuel range (€465–500) rather than a fixed
  figure and flags parking as a separate suggested add-on (~€30–40); this
  budget uses the upper bound of both to stay conservative.
- `food.md` flags an unbudgeted ~€5–10/day discretionary buffer (drinks/gelato
  beyond the dinner picks) that is not included in its €565 total and is not
  added here — covered only incidentally by the 10% contingency, not a
  guaranteed match.
- `accommodation.md` rates and `transport.md` rental/fuel rates are both
  explicitly flagged as unconfirmed, date-sensitive estimates (peak July
  pricing) — actual costs at booking time may vary from the figures above.
- No flight costs are included, per the requirements.md scope (budget excl. flights).
