# Route — Tuscany 2026-07 (5-Day Loop, Florence → Florence)

## Assumptions
- Loop confirmed by requirements.md (fly in/out of Florence).
- Base towns chosen to balance culture (Siena, San Gimignano) with countryside/outdoor
  (Chianti, Val d'Orcia), per execution-plan.md's "culture/outdoor balance" and
  "kid-friendly pacing" goals.
- One-night stays kept to a minimum (only 1) to reduce daily driving and re-packing
  stress with young kids; longer stay anchors (Siena, Val d'Orcia) allow half-day
  countryside excursions with minimal driving.
- Exact hotel addresses within each town are set by the accommodation agent; distances
  below are town-center to town-center and may vary ±5–10 min based on the specific
  hotel.

## Rationale

- **Loop shape honored**: route departs and returns to Florence as required, with no
  backtracking through the same town twice (Florence → Chianti → Siena → Val d'Orcia →
  San Gimignano → Florence forms a clean clockwise loop through central Tuscany).
- **Drive-time cap (QG2)**: every leg is ≤1h40min, well inside the ≤2.5h/day ceiling,
  leaving buffer for rest stops, wrong turns, and kid bathroom breaks — important for
  self-drive with a 6- and 9-year-old.
- **Kid pacing**: no single day combines a long drive with a packed sightseeing
  schedule. Travel days (1 and 5) have the shortest legs so kids arrive with energy
  left; the longest drive (Day 4) is scheduled between two lower-key countryside/small-
  town stops rather than after a big city day.
- **Culture/outdoor balance**: Siena and San Gimignano deliver the culture/history
  component (piazzas, towers, Duomo); Chianti and Val d'Orcia/Pienza deliver
  countryside, vineyards, and outdoor scenery — alternating rather than clustering,
  so the trip doesn't front-load or back-load either theme.
- **One-night-per-stop tradeoff**: with only 5 days, a strict "2 nights per base" plan
  would force either a 2-town loop (limiting variety) or legs approaching the cap on
  changeover days. This route accepts 4 single-night stays to keep every drive short
  and give the family a different, walkable home base each night, while still avoiding
  same-day long-drive-plus-heavy-sightseeing combinations.
- **Handoff to downstream agents**: town list (Greve in Chianti, Siena, Pienza, San
  Gimignano, plus Florence bookend) is fixed here for accommodation, activities, food,
  and transport agents to build on; if any Group B agent surfaces a QG3–QG5 conflict
  tied to town choice, escalate back to route for resequencing per the execution plan's
  iteration strategy.
